About Me

My name is Joseph and I am a ameteur developer and IT guy. I mainly develop games but have tried my hand at web design (obviously, since you're on this page!). I enjoy programming challenges and especially like to develop in unconvential ways.

I've loved computers for literally my entire life, and have been dabbling in programming of some kind since elementary school, albeit it was mostly scratch. Realistically, though, I have been programming for about 3 years and am mainly proficient in Java; however, I also have some experience in C# and Python. I know about enough HTML/CSS to develop this page but am certainly no web developer.

The last two years I have worked as an IT Intern at my highschool. I mostly fix chromebooks -- but I occasionally get to do some actual IT work in a teacher's classroom. In my free time, I like to repair and/or mod consoles I get from my business.

Speaking of which, I've been collecting videos games since about when I was born as well! It started with V-Smiel and later into old nintendo and playstation. Now I do a bit of everything. I think in middle school is when I turned this hobby into a business where I resell games at sidewalk sales, conventions, and recently, ebay. I have so. many. games.
